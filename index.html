Explore Sunset Beach

🏖️ Sunset Beach

Just a short 4-minute walk away, Sunset Beach is known for its wide sandy shoreline, breathtaking sunsets, and peaceful atmosphere. Perfect for swimming, shell hunting, and family beach days.

📬 Kindred Spirit Mailbox

One of North Carolina's most beloved attractions. Take a walk along Bird Island and leave a note in the famous Kindred Spirit Mailbox while enjoying untouched coastal scenery.

⛳ Championship Golf

Play some of the best courses on the Carolina coast. More than 30 golf courses are located within a short drive of the property, including several nationally recognized layouts.

🎣 Fishing & Boating

Bring your boat and enjoy direct canal access from the private dock. Fish, cruise the Intracoastal Waterway, or spend the day exploring nearby waterways and inlets.

🦀 Calabash Seafood

Experience the seafood capital of the Carolinas. Calabash is just minutes away and offers fresh local seafood, waterfront dining, and charming coastal shops.

🎡 Myrtle Beach

Looking for more excitement? Myrtle Beach is only about 30 minutes away and offers shopping, entertainment, attractions, nightlife, and family activities.

🛍️ Local Dining & Shopping

Discover the charm of Sunset Beach and nearby Calabash with locally owned restaurants, waterfront seafood dining, ice cream shops, beach boutiques, gift stores, and seasonal markets. Whether you're searching for fresh seafood or unique coastal treasures, you'll find plenty to explore just minutes away.

🌉 Swing Bridge Museum

Step back in time at the historic Sunset Beach Swing Bridge & Museum. The original 1958 bridge has been preserved as a local landmark featuring exhibits, artifacts, photographs, and stories that celebrate the rich history of Sunset Beach and the Carolina coast.
